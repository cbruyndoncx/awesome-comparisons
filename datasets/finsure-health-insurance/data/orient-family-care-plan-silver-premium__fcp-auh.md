# Orient Family Care Plan - Silver Premium (Abu Dhabi)

The Orient Silver Premium is a family care plan for Abu Dhabi and Al Ain with worldwide territorial coverage and an annual aggregate limit of AED 1,000,000. The plan includes dental coverage up to AED 3,500 and features a deductible of AED 50 plus 10% copay on outpatient pharmacy.
