# Orient Family Care Plan - Gold (Dubai)

The Gold plan is a worldwide family care plan offered by Orient Insurance PJSC with an annual aggregate limit of AED 1,000,000. It includes dental coverage of AED 3,500 and features a 20% deductible up to a maximum of AED 50, plus 10% copay on outpatient diagnostics and pharmacy.

### Description

Dental benefits are capped at AED 3,500, and an additional AED 37 plus VAT applies for Dubai visa holders.
