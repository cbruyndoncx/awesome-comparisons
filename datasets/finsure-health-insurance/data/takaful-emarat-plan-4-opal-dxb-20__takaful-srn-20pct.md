### Description
- Takaful-compliant health insurance with worldwide territorial scope and AED 1 million aggregate annual coverage. NAS-SRN network with private room inpatient accommodation and 20% coinsurance structure across most services. Pre-existing conditions covered up to AED 150,000 per year subject to declaration and medical underwriting with no waiting period. Maternity covered up to AED 12,500 with 10% coinsurance, dental AED 1,500 with 20% copay, optical AED 1,000 with detailed sub-limits, and dialysis AED 60,000 with 20% copay per visit. Direct billing within network settled at 80% of usual and customary rates; outside network reimbursement varies by NAS presence (100% where absent, 80% where present).

## Rating

### Reason
- The plan offers worldwide coverage and reasonable aggregate limit but applies 20% coinsurance broadly, caps pre-existing conditions at AED 150,000 (below aggregate limit), and provides moderate dental/optical/alternative medicine sub-limits typical of mid-tier plans. Maternity coverage requires declaration and medical underwriting with potential premium adjustment for undeclared pregnancies.
