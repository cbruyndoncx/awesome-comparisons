# Orient Family Care Plan - Silver Premium (Dubai)

The Silver Premium plan is a worldwide family care plan offered by Orient Insurance PJSC with an annual aggregate limit of AED 1,000,000. The plan includes dental coverage of AED 3,500 and features a 20% deductible up to a maximum of AED 50, plus 20% copay on outpatient diagnostics and pharmacy.

### Description
- Orient Insurance PJSC's Silver Premium is a worldwide family care plan designed for Dubai and other Northern Emirates residents. The plan offers an annual aggregate limit of AED 1,000,000 with dental coverage capped at AED 3,500. It features a 20% deductible structure up to a maximum of AED 50, along with 20% copayment requirements on outpatient diagnostics and pharmacy services. Pricing excludes VAT, with an additional AED 37 plus VAT applicable for Dubai visa holders.
