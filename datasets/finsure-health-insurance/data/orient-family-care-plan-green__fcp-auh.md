# Orient Family Care Plan - Green (Abu Dhabi)

The Family Care Plan - Green is a worldwide coverage plan offered by Orient Insurance PJSC for Abu Dhabi and Al Ain residents. It provides an annual aggregate limit of AED 1,000,000 with a deductible of AED 50 and 30% copay on outpatient pharmacy. The plan includes dental coverage of AED 3,500 across all tiers.
