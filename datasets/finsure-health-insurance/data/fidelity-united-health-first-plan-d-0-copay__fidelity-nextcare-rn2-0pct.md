### Description
- Worldwide coverage individual health plan with AED 1 million annual limit on NEXTCARE RN2 network, featuring private inpatient rooms and 20% outpatient copay capped at AED 50. Pre-existing conditions covered up to AED 150,000 per member per year subject to declaration and medical underwriting. Maternity benefits up to AED 10,000 with 10% coinsurance, dental covered up to AED 3,500 with 20% copay, and dialysis covered up to AED 60,000 per year with 20% copay. Direct billing within network, reimbursement at 80% UCR outside network within UAE, and full reimbursement (subject to UCR) for treatment outside UAE up to 90 days per trip.

## Rating

### Overall Quality
- [3] Mid-range plan with capped pre-existing limit of AED 150,000 and moderate maternity/dental sub-limits

### Reason
- The plan offers worldwide coverage with a respectable AED 1 million aggregate limit and comprehensive benefits including organ transplant (AED 100,000 sub-limit), but the pre-existing condition cap of AED 150,000 and mandatory medical underwriting requirement position it as a mid-tier product. Maternity and dental sub-limits are moderate (AED 10,000 and AED 3,500 respectively), while the 20% outpatient copay structure with AED 50 cap provides reasonable cost-sharing. The 0% copay on prescribed drugs is a positive feature, but overall the coverage parameters and sub-limits align with a middle-market positioning rather than premium comprehensive coverage.
