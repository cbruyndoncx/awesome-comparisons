# Orient NEMED-Lite

NEMED-Lite is a basic health insurance plan offered by Orient Insurance PJSC covering the UAE excluding Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Alain, plus the insured's home country. The plan features a 12-month waiting period for maternity benefits (ages 18-45) and uses PCP network for outpatient care with RN3 network for inpatient services. Premium structure varies by age bracket with coverage available up to age 76+.
