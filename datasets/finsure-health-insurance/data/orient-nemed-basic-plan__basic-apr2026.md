### Description
- NEMED-Basic is a basic-tier health insurance plan from Orient Insurance PJSC covering the UAE excluding Dubai, Abu Dhabi and Alain. The plan requires a 12-month waiting period and serves all age categories from 0 to 76+. Network access is through PCP-NE for both outpatient and inpatient services. Premium rates are tiered by age with 0-65 at AED 290.17, 66-75 at AED 1,031.51, and 76+ at AED 1,321.85 inclusive of all charges and VAT.
