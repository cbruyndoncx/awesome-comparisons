# Orient DMED-LSB

DMED-LSB is a basic-tier health insurance plan offered by Orient Insurance PJSC, designed for dependents of employees earning up to AED 4,000 per month. The plan provides coverage across the UAE and the insured's home country, with outpatient services through PCP network and inpatient care via RN3 network.
