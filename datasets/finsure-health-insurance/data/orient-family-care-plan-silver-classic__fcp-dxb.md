# Orient Family Care Plan - Silver Classic (Dubai)

The Silver Classic plan is a worldwide coverage health insurance option offered by Orient Insurance PJSC with an annual aggregate limit of AED 1,000,000. The plan includes dental benefits up to AED 3,500 and is designed for families in Dubai and the Northern Emirates. A 20% deductible applies up to a maximum of AED 50, with 0% copay on outpatient diagnostics and pharmacy.
