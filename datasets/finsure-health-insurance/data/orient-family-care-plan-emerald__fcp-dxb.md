# Orient Family Care Plan - Emerald (Dubai)

The Emerald plan is part of Orient Insurance's Family Care Plan series for Dubai and Northern Emirates, offering worldwide coverage with an annual aggregate limit of AED 1,000,000. The plan includes AED 3,500 dental coverage and requires an additional AED 37 + VAT for Dubai visa holders.
