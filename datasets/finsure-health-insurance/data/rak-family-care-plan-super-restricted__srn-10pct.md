# RAK Family Care Plan - Super Restricted

The RAK Family Care Plan - Super Restricted offers worldwide coverage with a AED 1,000,000 annual aggregate limit on the NAS Super Restricted network. The plan provides private room hospitalization and includes a AED 150,000 sub-limit for declared pre-existing conditions. Maternity coverage is capped at AED 10,000 with a 10% co-payment on all maternity treatments.

### Description
- This plan provides worldwide coverage with a AED 1,000,000 annual limit on a Super Restricted network basis. Pre-existing conditions are covered up to AED 150,000 if declared and underwritten. Maternity benefits include AED 10,000 for normal delivery and caesarean with 10% member co-payment. Dental coverage is capped at AED 3,500 with 20% co-pay, and dialysis is limited to AED 60,000. Outpatient consultations carry a 20% co-pay with AED 50 maximum per visit.

## Rating

### Overall Quality
- [3] Mid-range coverage with worldwide scope and private room standard, but restricted by Super Restricted network and moderate sub-limits for maternity (AED 10,000), dental (AED 3,500), and dialysis (AED 60,000).

### Reason
- The plan offers a high annual aggregate limit (AED 1,000,000) and worldwide coverage, which are strong features. However, the Super Restricted network significantly limits provider choice, and key benefits have relatively low sub-limits: maternity is capped at AED 10,000 per event, dental at AED 3,500 annually, and dialysis at AED 60,000. Pre-existing conditions are covered but only up to AED 150,000 and require declaration and underwriting. The 20% outpatient co-pay (max AED 50) and 10% maternity co-pay add member cost-sharing. Overall, this is a mid-tier plan suitable for families seeking basic comprehensive coverage but willing to accept network restrictions and moderate sub-limits.
