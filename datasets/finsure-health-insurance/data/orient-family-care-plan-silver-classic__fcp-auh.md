# Orient Family Care Plan - Silver Classic (Abu Dhabi)

The Orient Silver Classic is a family care plan available in Abu Dhabi and Al Ain with worldwide coverage and an annual aggregate limit of AED 1,000,000. The plan includes a dental benefit of AED 3,500 and features a deductible structure with AED 50 plus 10% copay on outpatient pharmacy services. Final rates are subject to underwriting and released through Orient's online portal.
