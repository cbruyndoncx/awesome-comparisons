# Orient Family Care Plan - Emerald (Abu Dhabi)

The Family Care Plan - Emerald is a worldwide coverage plan offered by Orient Insurance PJSC for Abu Dhabi and Al Ain residents. It provides an annual aggregate limit of AED 1,000,000 with dental coverage up to AED 3,500. The plan includes a AED 50 deductible and 30% copayment on outpatient pharmacy services.
