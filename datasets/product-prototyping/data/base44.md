### Description
Base44 is an AI-powered application development platform that enables users to create custom, fully-functional web apps without writing any code. Following a June 2025 acquisition by Wix for $80 million, the platform launched Base44 2.0 on October 30, 2025 as an all-in-one app building solution. Loved by over 40,000 builders globally, Base44 revolutionizes app development through conversational, prompt-driven building with iterative edits. The platform features a flexible AI engine that auto-selects between Claude Sonnet 4 and Gemini 2.5 Pro, with built-in version history and a discussion mode for ideation without affecting the live app.
