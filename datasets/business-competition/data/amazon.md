## General Info

### Classification
- Cloud Platform

### Short Description
- AWS provides the largest hyperscale cloud with deep infrastructure, developer tooling, and partner marketplace coverage.

### Description
AWS combines granular infrastructure primitives, managed databases, analytics, AI/ML tooling, and application services. Enterprises leverage its maturity, marketplace ecosystem, and operational rigor for core modernization, all while Amazon drives up-stack plays with Bedrock, Q, and industry solutions.

## SWOT

### Strengths
- Market leader with economies of scale; unmatched partner ecosystem; strong security/compliance posture

### Weaknesses

### Opportunities
- GenAI platform (Bedrock+Q), industry solutions, private 5G/edge monetization, sustainability tooling

### Threats
- Multi-cloud mandate reduces wallet share; competition from Azure + Google; regulatory scrutiny over dominance
